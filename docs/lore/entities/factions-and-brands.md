# Factions And Brands

AFK Raiders groups should be broad social jokes, not deep political lore. They exist to generate comms flavor, item labels, vendor personalities, and hub gossip.

Old-world brands should feel like they survived as packaging, signage, bad warranties, and objects that still believe in customer satisfaction.

Recurring vendor voices live in [Traders](traders.md). Add a trader there when a faction or brand becomes a repeated desk, kiosk, or future shop personality.

## Survivor Groups

| Group | Canon identity | Content hooks |
|---|---|---|
| Coupon Barons | Traders who treat discount math like statecraft. | Vendor jokes, stash sales, hub gossip. |
| Blue-Tarp Nomads | Surface wanderers who can build shelter from anything except a good decision. | Zone events, field rumors, low-danger survival flavor. |
| Clipboard Researchers | People studying dangerous things at exactly the wrong distance. | Strange markings, machine observations, failed field notes. |
| Gear Weirdos | Loadout sellers with too much confidence in refurbished equipment. | Future store content, shield and recharger jokes. |
| Permit Choir | Desperanza clerks who recite rules in unison when frightened. | Hub bureaucracy, Ready Up friction. |
| Locker Philosophers | Raiders who consider hiding a tactical worldview. | Hiding in Lockers progression, hub comedy. |
| Snack Shrine Custodians | Informal caretakers of emergency food piles that may or may not be cursed. | Mood events, consumables, field caches. |

## Writing Rules

- Factions should be useful as flavor tags, not mandatory homework.
- Brands should be funny even if the player never reads this page.
- Avoid making groups too competent; AFK Raiders thrives on people doing their best with terrible systems.
- Add recurring groups or brands here before using them repeatedly in runtime content.
